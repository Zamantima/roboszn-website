Our Mission

To empower the next generation of innovators in Secunda and ultimately South Africa with coding, robotics, and AI skills.

Our Story

RoboSZN was created to fill a critical gap in communities like Secunda, where learners have passion, potential, and creativity, but limited access to future-focused tech education. We started with a simple idea: give kids the tools to code, build, and solve real problems while having fun and leveling up.
