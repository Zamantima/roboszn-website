Why RoboSZN?

RoboSZN is a coding and robotics club for kids in Grades 1 to 12 designed to develop future-ready skills through hands-on projects, competitions, and real-world challenges. With a unique gamified and season-based learning journey—SeedSZN to FutureSZN—we teach coding, robotics, and an emphasis on AI in a fun, progressive, and accessible way.

Our Mission

To empower the next generation of innovators in South Africa with coding, robotics, and AI skills—one season at a time.

Our Story

RoboSZN was created to fill a critical gap in communities like Secunda—where learners have passion, potential, and creativity, but limited access to future-focused tech education. We started with a simple idea: give kids the tools to code, build, and solve real problems—while having fun and leveling up like they do in games.

Our Core Values

  • 🤖 Hands-On Learning – Kids learn by doing, not memorizing
  • 🧠 Real-World Relevance – AI, electronics, robotics
  • 🚀 Gamified Progression – XP, badges, and real rewards
  • 🎯 Inclusive & Encouraging – Especially for girls & first-time tech learners

📍 Programs by RoboSZN

🌱 SeedSZN

Students get introduced to logic, creativity, and the joy of solving problems.

  • 🎨 Skills: Design Thinking, Storytelling
  • 🧩 Tools: Scratch Jr, Paper Prototyping
  • 🎓 Output: Digital Stories, Game Ideas

🛠️ BuildSZN

Hands-on building with real-world kits and creative circuits.

  • ⚙️ Skills: Engineering Basics, Spatial Thinking
  • 🧰 Tools: LEGO SPIKE Prime, Arduino Basics
  • 🎓 Output: Physical Models & Mini Robots

💻 CodeSZN

Dive into real coding logic and software systems.

  • 🔁 Skills: Loops, Conditions, Functions
  • 🖥 Tools: Scratch, Python, TinkerCAD
  • 🎓 Output: Games, Simulations, Virtual Circuits

🏆 CompeteSZN

Students apply what they’ve learned in friendly challenges and competitions.

  • 🎯 Skills: Teamwork, Strategy, Presentation
  • 🤖 Platforms: WRO, Local Robotics Leagues
  • 🎓 Output: Robotics Matches, Team Projects

🤖 FutureSZN

Advanced AI thinking, real-world problems, and future careers.

  • 🧠 Skills: AI, Data Thinking, Automation
  • 📱 Tools: ChatGPT, Teachable Machine, Sensors
  • 🎓 Output: Smart Projects, AI-Inspired Builds
